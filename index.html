About

I'm Anton Waller, founder of WALLTAIR. I work as an independent technical consultant, taking on assignments primarily through consulting brokers on a pure-referral basis — no staffing markup, no middle layer between the work and the client.

My background sits at the intersection of engineering documentation and practical implementation: turning ambiguous technical requirements into systems, drawings, and processes that hold up under real use. That's the same lens I bring to Cortona3D projects, process improvement work, and the automation tooling I build alongside it.

REF 03

Why Cortona3D

Cortona3D turns static manuals into interactive 3D work instructions and IETPs — the kind of documentation that actually shortens training time and reduces errors on the floor. It's a narrow, specialized skill set, which is exactly why it's in short supply.

WALLTAIR is built around that specialization: implementation, configuration, and content authoring for teams that need technical documentation to do more than sit in a binder.
